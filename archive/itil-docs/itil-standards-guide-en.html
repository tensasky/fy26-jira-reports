L1 SERVICE TEAM METRICS

L1 Service Team KPIs

Service Desk
  • Incident Response Time < 15 minutes
  • First Contact Resolution Rate > 75%
  • Service Request Fulfillment SLA > 90%
  • Customer Satisfaction Score > 4.2/5
  • Average Handle Time optimization
  • Ticket Escalation Rate < 20%
Key Focus Areas
  • Reduce mean time to acknowledge (MTTA)
  • Improve first call resolution (FCR)
  • Enhance self-service adoption rate
  • Standardize ticket categorization
  • Strengthen knowledge base utilization
  • Implement proactive monitoring alerts
